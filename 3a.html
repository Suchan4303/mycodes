Cars

A car is a wheeled motor vehicle used for transportation. It is designed to run on roads and typically has four wheels, with an internal combustion engine as the power source.

Types of Cars

  1. Sedan


    Sedans are passenger cars with a separate trunk for cargo.A sedan is defined as a 4-door passenger car with a trunk that is separate from the passengers with a three-box body: the engine, the area for passengers, and the trunk. Throughout the generations, the definition of a sedan has been the same. Many sedans do offer a fold-down rear seatback that opens a pass-through to accommodate large objects that wouldn't normally fit in the trunk. But with the seatback in its upright position, the trunk is sealed off from access and prying eyes.

  2. SUV


    SUVs, or sport utility vehicles, are larger vehicles with a spacious interior and off-road capabilities.A sport utility vehicle (SUV) is a car classification that combines elements of road-going passenger cars with features from off-road vehicles, such as raised ground clearance and four-wheel drive.SUV is a loose term that traditionally covers a broad range of vehicles with four-wheel drive that can be taken off-road and have the feel of a lighter truck.

  3. Hatchback


    Hatchbacks have a rear door that opens upwards to provide access to the cargo area.The hatch is typically a liftgate with hinges on the top, so it is simple to open and pass cargo through.
